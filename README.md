## Staircase Risk Elicitation Task (Falk et al, 2016)

This oTree app allows facilitates the implementation of the 'staircase' risk elicitation task --- as proposed by Falk et al. (2016), Working Paper --- in different variants and parameterizations by simply altering the documented variables in config.py.
